From sourcing the finest yarns to curating stunning designs, we pour our passion into every thread. Whether you're a seasoned crafter, a budding designer, or simply looking for the perfect textile to elevate your home, Mariya Textiles offers a diverse and inspiring collection. Our Services

Mariya Textiles likely offers these key services:

* **Fabric Sales (Retail & Wholesale):** Selling a variety of fabrics to individual customers and businesses.

* **Custom Fabric Sourcing:** Finding specific fabrics not readily available in stock.

* **Textile Design & Printing:** Creating custom designs and printing them on fabrics.

* **Sewing & Alterations:** Offering sewing services for garments and home textiles, including alterations.

* **Embroidery & Embellishment:** Adding embroidery or other embellishments to fabrics and garments.

* **Textile Consultation:** Providing expert advice on fabric selection and usage.

* **Garment Manufacturing (Potentially):** Creating finished garments based on client designs and specifications (depending on scale).

* **Home Textile Products:** Selling ready-made curtains, bedding, and other home textile items.
